HashAwaaz

Idea about a very simple interface for those who cannot read or write. Right now people who are educated can easily voice their opinions through social media but what about the portion of the world who cannot?

The cost of smartphones in developing countries like Pakistan has been rapidly decreasing. This has opened opportunities for new interface interactions. This is a concept of a simple visual interface for the illiterate with icons representing services to problems they encounter frequently like Crime.

Upon choosing one of the categories, they will then be given three options. Explaining this through an example, lets say there is a lot of garbage accumulating in an area in Rural Pakistan which is causing health risks. Resident there can communicate with the authorities by taking a picture and recording a message through which they can explain the situation. The hashtag feature is meant to be one word recordings describing/categorizing their situation.Words spoken once have more clarity than full sentences which will help us translate them in text form and then map them out easily to visualize general problems in various areas.

Above is a quick mockup of a web dasboard which authorities can have access which will give a feed of user submissions. The spoken words in the hashtag sections can then be translated into text mapped out according to location which will be captured by the App. The same way pictures can then be displayed in Pinterest style to show what is happening around regions.

INSAAF

Platform that tracks human right violations

Idea about a Human Rights violation platform called Insaaf which in urdu means Justice. Numerous human rights violation occur in Pakistan, some of which are picked up by Media for example the infamous Shahzeb killing case in Karachi, Pakistan (Shahzeb Case). But eventually in such cases the families of victims are pressurized into pardoning the perpetrators. After sometime the story becomes old and people forget about it, and then the same crime is commited again by someone else who thinks that they can beat the system. The way the platform will work is that users will create a submission of a human rights violation which can then be tracked by others. Every violation will have its own profile with description and timeline. Users will be able to comments on the current status of what is happening and they can also come together to create actions assigned to volunteering users for solving these problems and demand justice.

Taleem

Idea of a way to educate the illiterate through images which they can understand and then relate to text words

Images are provided with a letter on top indicating start of the word. As many can understand what images means therefore it could serve as a reference point to learning letters and words.

Once an image is clicked, it then displays the word and also a voice recording of the pronounciation of the word which can then be picked up by the person.
